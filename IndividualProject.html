Agent Profile


In every detail of your real estate transaction, Lindsey embodies integrity, energy, hard effort, and imaginative service. Lindsey was raised in New Jersey and began working at the age of 13 when she and a neighbourhood buddy launched a local dog walking business. She has maintained her early morning routine and has now established a thriving real estate profession. She has represented sellers, purchasers, investors, and banks in the residential market and has worked in every element of the sector. With over 8 years of experience, specializing in short-sale, relocation, as a listing and buying agent, Lindsey's company model revolves around customer satisfaction. Her personal objectives have continually set her aside from the crowd, allowing her to confidently finish all of her clients' transactions in a timely and flawless manner.

Lindsey began her real estate career in Toronto and has since been recruited by RE/MAX The Real Estate Leaders in Ontario, one of Canada's best real estate offices. This decision has put her in the company of several great real estate producers and has contributed significantly to her career.
